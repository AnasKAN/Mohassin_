Who Are We?

At Mohassin, we are dedicated to revolutionizing large-scale event management through cutting-edge optimization technologies. Rooted in Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030, our mission is to streamline crowd management and transportation solutions, ensuring the safety, efficiency, and satisfaction of millions of pilgrims who gather annually for Hajj. By providing Optimization as a Service (OaaS), Mohassin offers an integrable platform where complex operational challenges are met with tailored optimization solutions. Through our centralized API, we empower researchers, planners, and organizations to solve dynamic problems with precision, saving time and resources while enhancing the overall experience of large-scale events. With scalability, adaptability, and efficiency as our core principles, Mohassin stands as a beacon for innovation, designed to handle today's challenges and adapt to tomorrow's possibilities. Together, we aim to create seamless, data-driven solutions for the world's most significant gatherings​
